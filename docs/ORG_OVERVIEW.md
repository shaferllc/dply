# Organization overview

The **Overview** is the home of an organization workspace. It gives you an at‑a‑glance picture of the organization and quick links to everything you can manage from here.

## What an organization is

An organization is the top‑level container for your infrastructure. Servers, sites, credentials, billing, members, and teams all belong to an organization. The same person can belong to several organizations and have a different role in each.

The left sidebar is your map of the organization:

| Section | What lives there |
| --- | --- |
| **Overview** | This page — summary and quick links |
| **General** | Name, icon, region defaults, and other organization settings |
| **Activity** | Audit trail of everything that changes |
| **Automation** | API tokens, webhooks, and notification defaults |
| **Billing & plan** | Subscription, plan limits, and payment method |
| **Billing analytics / Invoices** | Usage breakdowns and downloadable invoices |
| **Members** | People, roles, and pending invitations |
| **Teams** | Groups of members for scoped access and notifications |
| **Server providers** | Cloud provider credentials (DigitalOcean, Hetzner, …) |
| **Notification channels** | Where alerts are delivered |
| **Webserver templates** | Reusable web server config presets |

## Quick links

The Overview surfaces shortcut tiles to the sections you have permission to open. What you see depends on your role — admins and owners see the full set, while members and deployers see a subset.

## Roles at a glance

Most of what you can do here is gated by your organization role. See **[Organization roles & plan limits](org-roles-and-limits)** for the full breakdown of owner, admin, member, and deployer, plus how trial versus Pro affects server and site limits.

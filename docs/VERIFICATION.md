# Email verification

Email verification is **required** for all authenticated use of the app. You must verify your email before you can create or manage servers, organizations, credentials, billing, or team membership.

## How it works

- When you register, a verification link is sent to your email address.
- Until you verify, any attempt to open the dashboard, servers, organizations, credentials, profile, or other protected areas will redirect you to the **Verify email** page.
- On that page you can:
  - **Resend verification email** – Click the button to send a new link (e.g. if the first one expired or didn’t arrive).
  - **Log out** – Leave the app and sign back in later after verifying.

## Resend and verify

1. Go to the **Verify email** page (you are sent there automatically if you’re logged in but not verified).
2. Click **Resend Verification Email** to send a new link to your address.
3. Check your inbox (and spam) and click the link in the email.
4. After clicking the link, you’ll be marked as verified and can use the app normally.

Resend is rate-limited (e.g. a few attempts per minute) to prevent abuse.

## Why it’s required

Verification ensures that sensitive actions (creating servers, managing organizations, credentials, billing, and team membership) are tied to a real, reachable email address. It reduces abuse and keeps account recovery and notifications reliable.

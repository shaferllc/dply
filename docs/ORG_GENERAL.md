# General settings

The **General** page holds the organization's identity and workspace‑wide defaults. Only owners and admins can change these.

## Identity

- **Name** — how the organization is labelled across the app, in breadcrumbs, and on invitations.
- **Icon** — an optional logo shown next to the name in the sidebar and organization switcher. When no icon is set, dply shows the organization's initials.

## Defaults

Settings here apply to the whole organization rather than to a single server or site. Depending on your plan and configuration these may include regional defaults and other workspace‑level preferences that new resources inherit.

## Who can edit

| Role | Can edit General |
| --- | --- |
| **Owner** | Yes |
| **Admin** | Yes |
| **Member / Deployer** | No (read‑only or hidden) |

Deleting the organization is an **owner‑only** action and lives in its own danger zone, separate from everyday settings.

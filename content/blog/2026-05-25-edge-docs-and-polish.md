After yesterday's 29-commit Edge avalanche, today was the cooldown lap. Three
commits, mostly spent making sure the stuff I'd thrown down was actually usable
rather than just present.

A decent share of it was **docs** — when you ship a routing tab, SSR adapters, and
an import flow in one day, the documentation is always a day behind, and Edge has
enough moving parts now that "figure it out from the UI" stopped being a fair ask.
So I wrote some of it down while it was still fresh in my head.

The rest landed in the site workspace views and a little config tidying:

- Polish on the site workspace UI where yesterday's features had rough edges —
  spacing, copy, the small things you only notice once you stop building and start
  clicking.
- A config touch-up, the kind of follow-through that should've been part of the
  original change but never is.

There's a particular feeling to the day after a big sprint: half of it is admiring
the new thing and half is quietly fixing the parts you were too in-the-flow to get
right the first time. Today was mostly the second half, and that's fine.

Nothing dramatic to report, which after yesterday is its own kind of win. Back to
building something pointable tomorrow.
